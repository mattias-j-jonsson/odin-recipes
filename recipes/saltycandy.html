Salty Liquorice

Description

Widely recognized as the pinnacle of all that is good in this world!

Ingredients

  • blacker pepper
  • coal drenched in nitroglycerin
  • brain of a black widow
  • heart filled by The Abyss
  • 7 black candles

Steps

  1. grind the pepper to a fine powder in the skull of a deceased sorcerer
  2. put the brain in a stainless steel bowl.
  3. light the coal on fire and throw from safe distance into the brain bowl
  4. mix the charred brain with pepper
  5. meditate in a circle of the candles and let the brain pepper mix be embraced by your blackened heart
  6. serve with sugar free soda
