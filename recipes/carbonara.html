Pasta Carbonara

Description

A classic! This recipe gives you the creamy and authentic original italian dish.

Ingredients

  • egg yolks
  • black pepper
  • pecorino
  • guanciale
  • spaghetti

Steps

  1. cook the pasta until "al dente"
  2. combine egg yolks, pecorino and pepper in a small bowl, and add a little hot pasta water
  3. cook sliced guanciale in a large pan
  4. remove pan from eat, and remove guanciale
  5. mix egg mixture and pasta with a little pasta water in the pan
  6. add the guanciale
